ChickTech Austin High School Kickoff Weekend 2018

Saturday, December 8th to Sunday, December 9th

ChickTech High School is a free program for 100 Austin-area high school girls. Each fall we have a kick off event over 2 days where the girls each attend 1 hands-on workshop. At this event, we will need volunteers to help with logistics, registrations, and workshops.

All volunteers who are 18 or older will need to get a $15 background check to volunteer with our girls. Volunteers will be provided breakfast (AM shift) or lunch (PM shift) and a free ChickTech Tshirt.
