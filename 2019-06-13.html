Our Presentation

Creating a Raspberry Pi Kubernetes Cluster

Matt Moen will be demoing (and possibly creating--assuming the demo fairies show us favor) a Kubernetes cluster with a handful of raspberry Pis. We'll cover some of the basics of Kubernetes for those barely familiar with it, and touching on some of its cooler features.

Extra

Chris Weaver has been working on a similar project this year and has offered to share some more about Linux clustering, Kubernetes. He also has a great little hardware solution for those interested in trying this out for themselves. It is called the MiniPiRack.

Check it out at https://www.minipirack.com/
